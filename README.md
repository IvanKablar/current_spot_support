## About

Current Spot is a WearOS companion app that shows:
- ⚡ Current electricity prices
- 📊 Price forecasts for today and tomorrow
- 💰 Cheapest time periods
- 🔄 Automatic sync between phone and watch

## Privacy

We respect your privacy:
- All data is stored locally on your devices
- No analytics or tracking
- No data sharing with third parties
- Read our full [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md)

## Support

Having issues? Please [create an issue](https://github.com/IvanKablar/current_spot_support/issues) and we'll help you out!

## Requirements

- Tibber account with API token
- WearOS smartwatch (Android 10+)
- Android phone (Android 8.0+)
