Why Current Spot instead of the official Tibber app?

The official Tibber app is great on your phone — but there is no Tibber app for Wear OS. Current Spot fills that gap, and goes beyond what you get on the phone.

⌚ Built for the wrist

Round-screen layouts, rotating-crown scrolling and watch-face complications – designed for Wear OS from day one, not a mobile app squeezed onto a small screen.

🔒 Fully private

No analytics, no advertising, no tracking. Your Tibber token is stored only on your devices and used directly against the official Tibber API.

🌐 Offline-first

Today's and tomorrow's prices are cached on the watch. Once fetched, the app keeps working even when your watch has no connectivity.

📡 Smart connectivity

Uses your phone as a low-power Bluetooth relay when paired, and switches to WiFi/LTE automatically when the phone is out of range.

Getting started in 4 steps

  1. 1. Install the apps

    Install Current Spot from Google Play on both your phone and your Wear OS watch. Both apps are in the same Play Store listing.

  2. 2. Get your Tibber access token

    Sign in at developer.tibber.com with your Tibber account and generate a personal access token. Make sure it has the home and real-time scopes enabled if you want live power.

  3. 3. Enter the token in the companion app

    Open the Current Spot companion app on your phone, paste the token, and pick the home you want to monitor. The app validates the token against Tibber's servers instantly.

  4. 4. Pair your watch

    Open Current Spot on the watch. It will automatically pick up the configuration from your phone over Bluetooth. Add the complication to your favorite watch face, or pin the tile for one-swipe access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Tibber account?

Yes, you need an active Tibber account to use this app. The app displays your personalized electricity prices from Tibber.

Is this an official Tibber app?

No, Current Spot is an unofficial, community-developed app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected with Tibber AS.

Which devices are supported?

Current Spot works on all Wear OS devices running Wear OS 3.0 or later, including Samsung Galaxy Watch 4, 5, 6, and 7 series, Google Pixel Watch, and other Wear OS smartwatches.

How do I connect my Tibber account?

Install the companion app on your phone, generate a personal access token at developer.tibber.com, and pair your watch via Bluetooth or WiFi.

Does it work without a phone?

Yes! Once configured, the watch can fetch prices directly via WiFi or LTE. For live energy data, the Bluetooth relay through the companion phone app provides a low-power alternative.

Is it free?

Current Spot is free to download and use. All core features are included at no cost. Optional donations unlock live-power complications and bonus watch faces.
