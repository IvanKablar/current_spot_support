Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Tibber account?

Yes, you need an active Tibber account to use this app. The app displays your personalized electricity prices from Tibber.

Is this an official Tibber app?

No, Current Spot is an unofficial, community-developed app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected with Tibber AS.

Which devices are supported?

Current Spot works on all Wear OS devices running Wear OS 3.0 or later, including Samsung Galaxy Watch 4, 5, 6, and 7 series, Google Pixel Watch, and other Wear OS smartwatches.

How do I connect my Tibber account?

Install the companion app "Current Spot Setup" on your phone, generate a personal access token at developer.tibber.com, and pair your watch via Bluetooth or WiFi.

Does it work without a phone?

Yes! Once configured, the watch can fetch prices directly via WiFi or LTE. For live energy data, the Bluetooth relay through the companion phone app provides a low-power alternative.

Is it free?

Current Spot is free to download and use. Core features like price display, graphs, and price lists are included at no cost.
