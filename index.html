Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Tibber account?

Yes, you need an active Tibber account to use this app. The app displays your personalized electricity prices from Tibber.

Is this an official Tibber app?

No, Current Spot is an unofficial, community-developed app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected with Tibber AS.

Which devices are supported?

Current Spot works on all Wear OS devices running Wear OS 3.0 or later (Android API 29+).

How do I connect my Tibber account?

You'll need to generate a personal access token from your Tibber account and enter it in the companion app on your phone, which then syncs with your watch.

Does it work without a phone?

The watch app can run standalone once configured, but you'll need the companion app on your phone for initial setup and token management.

Is it free?

Yes, Current Spot is completely free to use. No in-app purchases or subscriptions.
