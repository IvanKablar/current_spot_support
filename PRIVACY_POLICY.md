# Privacy Policy

**Current Spot - WearOS App**
Last updated: February 22, 2026

## 1. Data Collection and Storage

This app collects and stores the following data locally on your devices:

- **Tibber Access Token**: Your personal Tibber API access token is stored locally on your phone and synchronized to your WearOS watch
- **Home ID**: Your Tibber Home identifier for retrieving electricity prices
- **Electricity Price Data**: Current and forecasted electricity prices from Tibber API
- **App Preferences**: Your selected settings (e.g., price interval: hourly/15-minute)

**All data is stored exclusively on your devices.** No data is transmitted to third-party servers except as described below.

## 2. Third-Party Services

### Tibber API
- The app connects to the official Tibber API (api.tibber.com) to retrieve electricity prices
- Your access token is used to authenticate with Tibber's servers
- Data transmitted: Access token, Home ID
- Data received: Electricity prices, price levels, home information
- Purpose: Display current and forecasted electricity prices on your smartwatch
- Privacy Policy: https://tibber.com/de/privacy-policy

### Google Play Services
- WearOS Data Layer: Used to synchronize your access token and settings between phone and watch
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No user data is shared with Google beyond what's required for app functionality

## 3. Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for:
- Authenticating with Tibber API
- Displaying electricity prices on your WearOS watch
- Synchronizing settings between your phone and watch
- Providing app functionality

**We do not:**
- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Use your data for analytics or advertising
- Store your data on external servers
- Track your usage behavior

## 4. Data Security

- All data is stored in app-private storage protected by Android's application sandbox
- Communication with Tibber API uses HTTPS encryption
- Your access token is never logged or exposed in debug output

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You can delete all stored data at any time by:
- Uninstalling the app from your phone and watch
- Revoking the Tibber access token in your Tibber account settings

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the app
- **Delete**: Remove all data by uninstalling
- **Portability**: Export your token from app settings
- **Correction**: Update your token or home selection

## 6. Data Retention

- Data is retained only while the app is installed
- Uninstalling permanently deletes all data from your device
- We do not retain any data after uninstallation

## 7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on our repository.
